Important Note About AI

Understanding AI Limitations

While our AI tools are powerful, the quality of results heavily depends on your input:

  • AI responds best to clear, detailed prompts
  • Results may vary and might need multiple attempts
  • Human creativity and guidance are essential for best results
  • Always review and refine your prompts for better outcomes

Creative Tips

Writing Effective Prompts

  • Be specific about art style (e.g., "oil painting," "digital art")
  • Include lighting and atmosphere details
  • Specify perspective and composition
  • Mention color schemes and moods

Best Practices

  • Start with simple prompts and gradually add complexity
  • Save successful prompts for future reference
  • Experiment with different styles and settings
  • Use reference images when available
